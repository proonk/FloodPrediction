About Sarawak Flood Risk

Sarawak, Malaysia's largest state, is prone to flooding due to its tropical climate and extensive river systems. The main rivers include:

  • Rajang River (Sibu)
  • Sarawak River (Kuching)
  • Baram River (Miri)
  • Kemena River (Bintulu)
  • Lupar River (Sri Aman)
